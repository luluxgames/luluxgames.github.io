Introduction to Video Game Narratives

Final Degree Project · 2024 · Grade: 9.5 / 10

My final degree project explores the foundations of video game narrative, connecting dramaturgy, interactive storytelling and game design.

The research studies how mechanics, player agency and narrative structures combine to create meaningful interactive experiences. It also examines the MDA Framework as a tool for analysing and designing narrative-driven games.

As part of the project I collaborated with Didascalia V.C., an educational VR initiative developed together with the Complutense University of Madrid. My role focused on mentoring the narrative structure of the experience and exploring how storytelling can support learning.

This project reinforced my interest in narrative design as a bridge between writing, gameplay and player experience.

LEFTOVER · Animated Short

LEFTOVER tells the story of Yago, a shy nine-year-old boy living in a grey coastal town. His only true companion is Leftover, a creature assembled from discarded pieces of marine life and forgotten objects.

Through fantasy, the story explores autism during childhood, loneliness and the need to find someone who understands you.
