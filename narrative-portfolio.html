LEFTOVER - Fantastic Animation

In a grey, desolate village, lives Yago, a shy nine-year-old boy facing personal and social struggles. His only comfort is his companion Leftover — a creature built from scrap and remnants of sea animals, created by his scientist parents to help him cope with loneliness.

This story addresses childhood autism and the difficulties it entails.

Across the Cosmos - Comedy & Adventure Miniseries

Steve, a human astronaut, travels across the universe visiting different planets in each episode. He encounters eccentric creatures and bizarre events, which he solves with wit and spontaneity. A recurring gag: his left arm randomly slaps him in the face.

El Chico Trizas – Fantastic Realism

An adaptation inspired by Sarah Kane’s 4.48 Psychosis. I reshaped the text into a new dramatic form, influenced by the real experience of someone going through a psychotic episode. The result: a raw, urban-inspired narrative with street-like characters.
